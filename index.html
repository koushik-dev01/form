1. RECITALS & PARTIES

This "AGREEMENT" is made and entered into between the licensed motor carrier ("CARRIER") and QUICK FREIGHT LOGISTICS LLC ("BROKER"), a licensed broker of property authorized by the Federal Highway Administration, Docket No. MC# 1178473.

2. TERM AND TERMINATION

The term of this Agreement shall be for the period of one year and automatically renew year after year. Either party may terminate this Agreement upon fifteen (15) days written notice.

3. INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS

Carrier shall procure and maintain public liability insurance in at least the minimum amount required by the FMCSA. In addition, Carrier shall procure and maintain cargo insurance in the amount of $100,000 for loss and damage to property. The cargo insurance shall have no exclusions or restrictions that would not be accepted by the FMCSA.

4. SAFETY & COMPLIANCE

Carrier agrees to abide by all DOT Regulations and Requirements, including Vehicle Inspections, Safety Procedures, and Driver Logs. Trailers shall be clean, dry, free of defects, and shall NOT have been used to transport garbage, hazardous waste, solid waste, or toxic materials.

5. PAYMENT TERMS

Broker agrees to pay Carrier for transportation services within 30 days of receipt by Broker of Carrier's freight invoice, Bill of Lading, and signed Rate Confirmation.

6. NON-SOLICITATION

Carrier agrees that it will at no time during this agreement and for a period of one (1) year after termination, directly or indirectly, solicit, divert, back-solicit, or perform services for any account of Broker. If Carrier violates this, Carrier shall be liable for normal commissions Broker would have received.

7. SUBCONTRACT PROHIBITION

CARRIER shall not re-broker, co-broker, subcontract, assign, or transfer shipments to any other entity without prior written consent of BROKER. Violation of this prohibition shall nullify any liability limitations.

8. INDEMNIFICATION & INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR

Carrier is an independent contractor and not an employee of Broker. Carrier agrees to indemnify and hold harmless Broker from any claims arising out of the Carrier's operations.

9. APPENDIX A: FOOD SAFETY (FSMA)

  • Pre-Cooling: Carrier must pre-cool trailer to shipper's stipulated temperature.
  • Maintained Temp: Carrier must maintain temperature at all times.
  • Seals: Driver must verify seals and record numbers on BOL.
  • Sanitary Condition: Carrier must comply with the Sanitary Food Transportation Act.

10. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by and interpreted under the laws of the State of OHIO.
